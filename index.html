About Me

Originally from Seattle, I have 3 sibling- including a identical brother. In 2001 I graduated with a degree in Biology from the Univeristy of Washington. After graduting, I moved to Boulder to persue my passion for rock climbing.

I worked a as lab technician and climbed as much as possible. In 2013, my girlfriend and I moved to Knoxville, TN so she could attend PA School.

After three long years in the South, we decided to move back West. Salt Lake had the perfect mix of an affordable town with lots of access to the mountains.

Now I am starting a new chapter in life- coding.
